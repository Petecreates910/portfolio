Milano Auto Detaling

This is a personal project that I did for my roommates detailing business.

Design Brief

The client wanted a vintage logo that was minimalistic with his car as the icon logo that also spoke to his buusiness being fast and clean.

To capture a minimalistic vintage aesthetic, I incorporated a hand-drawn typeface that adds an authentic and timeless feel. For the color palette, I chose bold, high-contrast tones of red and blue to evoke a classic vintage style while also giving the logo a sporty, automotive-inspired look.
