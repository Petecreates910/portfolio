What is Monarch Marina?

Monarch Marina is a fictional marina resort concept I created, located in the heart of Southern California’s coast. Monarch Marina takes pride in offering high-end services tailored for boaters who value security, delicacy, care, and convenience—both at sail and off sail..

  • Professional Boat Security
  • On-Site maintence and mechanical serves
  • Expert detailing crews
  • Exclusive slip accommodations

Whether customers are launching out for a long voyage or docking in for a weekend getaway, Monarch Marina ensures peace of mind and top-tier service, making every visit effortless and enjoyable.

Design Goal

To design a brand that is luxurious but also has some friendliness to it that would be inviting trustful and express the business values.
