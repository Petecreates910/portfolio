ABOUT ME

I am a senior graphic designer studying at Pensacola Christian College with a passion for creating visually compelling designs that tell a story. My journey in design began with a fascination for how visuals can communicate ideas and emotions. Over the years, I have honed my skills in various design disciplines, including branding, web design, print media and layout design.

I am always exited to apply my design skills to more real-world projects. I believe that design is not just about aesthetics, but also about solving problems and enhancing user experiences. My goal is to create designs that are not only visually appealing but also functional and impactful. Dont be afraid to get in touch!
